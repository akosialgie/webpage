Apple

Manage apples by thinning excess fruit for larger yields, ensuring proper pollination, and protecting against pests/diseases with timely sprays. Prune for shape and airflow, and fertilize according to soil tests.

Avocado

Manage it by ensuring well-drained soil, regular watering during dry spells, and strategic pruning. Protect from frost and pests, and fertilize appropriately for optimal yield.

Banana

This is a banana plant, recognizable by its large leaves and developing fruit bunch. To manage bananas, ensure full sun, rich soil, and consistent watering. Remove suckers for single-stem growth, and support the bunch as it matures to prevent breakage.

Corn

Manage it by planting in well-drained soil, ensuring adequate sunlight, providing consistent water, applying balanced fertilizer, and controlling pests and weeds through integrated pest management.

Pineapple

This is a pineapple plant, characterized by its spiky leaves and central fruit. Manage pineapples by ensuring well-drained soil, full sun, and infrequent watering. Harvest when the fruit turns golden and emits a sweet aroma.

Dragon Fruit

Manage it by providing sturdy support, regular watering during growth, and well-draining soil. Prune for shape and harvest ripe fruits when their skin yields slightly.

Durian

This is a durian tree, known for its large, pungent fruit. Successful durian management involves careful pruning, consistent watering, balanced fertilization, vigilant pest/disease control, and timely harvesting to ensure optimal yield and fruit quality.

Rambutan

Manage it by providing full sun, well-drained soil, consistent watering, and support for heavy fruit. Regularly check for pests and diseases, and harvest when glossy.

Guava

This is a guava plant, recognizable by its elliptical leaves and white flowers. Manage guavas with regular pruning for shape and fruit production. Ensure well-drained soil, consistent watering, and fertilization. Protect from pests and diseases for a healthy yield.

Papaya

This is a papaya plant, recognizable by its large, palmate leaves and developing fruit. Manage papayas by ensuring well-drained soil, ample sunlight, and regular watering. Protect from frost and pests, and harvest ripe fruit when skin turns yellow-orange.

Mango

This is a mango tree, identifiable by its characteristic leaves and fruit. Manage mangoes by pruning for shape and yield, fertilizing for growth, irrigating regularly, controlling pests/diseases, and harvesting ripe fruit carefully.

Canistel

Canistel, or eggfruit, is a tropical tree producing sweet, custard-like fruit. Manage it by ensuring well-drained soil, regular watering during dry spells, and occasional pruning for shape and fruit production. Protect from frost and pests.
